Maintenance

To ensure maximum efficiency and the longevity of your units you must maintain and have them cleaned on a regular basis. General filter cleans are a necessity to keep the recycled air in your home clean. With dust and other unhealthy particles, you or your family could risk breathing in small particles of mould and dirt in your lungs. This is especially hazardous for those with breathing related health issues or prone to air-born allergies. Keep it clean and stay healthy.

Your air-conditioning unit can become quite dirty within 12 months or even less; thereby not providing you with the same fresh clean air you have become accustomed to. Moreover, your electricity bill also gets higher as the unit has to work significantly harder which can shorten the efficiency and the life of the air-conditioner. We clean all brands of air-conditioners in residential and commercial settings to the highest professional standards.

As many of you might be aware, there are many refrigerants, such as chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs) that severely damage the ozone layer, whilst other refrigerant gases are often dangerous and extremely potent greenhouse gases. In fact, one kilogram of the refrigerant R410a has the same greenhouse impact as two tonnes of carbon dioxide, which is the equivalent of running your car for six months.
